About the Speedie Corporation

The Speedie stores' were bought by the new owner AlGahyar in 2010. He owned three small gas station on three different locations, Grand Blvd, Compton ST, and Virginia ST. They all located in Saint Louis, MO. He sold the one on Virginia ST. because it was very old and needed too much work to get fixed.

Only one of the three stores was open, which is the Speedie Gas station on South Grand. It was only a small old store with old pumps. The new owner of the Speedie put big efforts to change it to a bigger, better looking, and more active store. During time, AlGahyar changed the old look & design pumps with New & Nice design pumps carrying this Speedie Logo. He didn't stop on that, but he kept upgrading his store, then, he got a big nice looking canopy with the Speedie LED light Logo on it.

A while after all the big efforts he gave to his little store, he made a big surprise to the neighborhood by opening the second Speedie Gas Station on Compton!! It was a big and good surprise for the people who live a little bit far a way from the Speedie on Grand Blvd. Most of the people were happy about it because they don't have to walk all that big distant to get their daily stuff again.

Then after one year, he made his second surprise by opening a car wash, The Speedie Car Wash. A nice looking Design store that match the other two Speedies, and also carry the Speedie Logo. The Speedie Car Wash is a good place where you can change your car's oil. Also its a good place to fix or check any mechanical damage with your car.
